Memories

Four years of moments we'll never forget

🎓

First Day

New city. New faces. Hostel rooms that smelled like instant noodles and anxiety. We had no idea what we were doing — and that was the best part.

💻

Lab Sessions

Debugging at midnight. Segmentation faults. That one program that finally compiled after 3 hours. The joy was unmatched.

🏆

Hackathons

36 hours of no sleep, bad coffee, and brilliant ideas. We didn't always win — but we always built something real.

🎉

College Fests

We rehearsed for 2 days and performed like we'd been doing it for years. The stage was ours.

☕

3AM Chai

Late night chai sessions that turned into 3am life conversations. The canteen — our real classroom.

🚀

Placements

The anxiety, the prep, the calls at midnight — "I got it!" Placement season was chaos and triumph all at once.

2022 — 2026 · CSE · Avanthi's

"We came as strangers,
we leave as family."

To everyone who was part of this — thank you. For the late nights, the shared meals, the panic before exams, the celebrations after. For being there when it was hard and making it feel easy.

We didn't just study together. We grew up together. And no matter where life takes us next, this chapter will always be the one we look back on and smile.
